🩺 Quick First Aid Guide

❤️ Cardiac Arrest

  1. Call 108 immediately
  2. Start CPR: 30 chest compressions
  3. Give 2 rescue breaths
  4. Use AED if available
  5. Continue until help arrives

🧠 Stroke (FAST)

  1. Face – drooping on one side?
  2. Arms – weakness/numbness?
  3. Speech – slurred or strange?
  4. Time – call 108 NOW
  5. Do NOT give food or water

🤧 Choking

  1. Ask "Are you choking?"
  2. 5 back blows between shoulder blades
  3. 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich)
  4. Alternate until object dislodges
  5. Call 108 if unresponsive

🔥 Burns

  1. Cool with running water 10 min
  2. Remove jewellery near burn
  3. Cover loosely with cling film
  4. Do NOT use ice or butter
  5. Seek medical attention
